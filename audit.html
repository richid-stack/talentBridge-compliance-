Common Questions

FAQs

Who is this audit for?
This audit is for African professionals — university students, freelancers, and early-career AI workers in Ghana, Nigeria, Kenya, Rwanda and beyond — who want to access EU and US remote AI roles.
What happens after I submit?
Within 24 hours, Richard will review your submission and send you a personalised compliance score along with your next steps. You'll also get access to your TBC dashboard where your passport lives.
Is it really free?
Yes. The Level 1 Audit and Compliance Passport are completely free. TBC earns a placement fee from deployers when you get hired — you never pay a cent.
What is the EU AI Act?
The EU AI Act is Europe's landmark law regulating artificial intelligence. Article 26 specifically requires human oversight (HITL) for high-risk AI systems. TBC certifies that you understand and can perform this role compliantly.
What roles can I access?
AI Red-Teamer, Prompt Engineer, Data Labeler/Annotator, HITL Specialist, Content Moderator, Virtual Assistant (AI tools), AI Trainer, and Quality Reviewer — all fully remote, paying €2,500–€3,500/month.
How is my data protected?
All data is stored in Firebase with Firestore security rules and encrypted in transit. Your data is processed under GDPR Article 6(1)(b) — necessary for the performance of a contract. You can request deletion at any time.
