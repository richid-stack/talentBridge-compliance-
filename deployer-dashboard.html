Compliance Hub

EU AI Act requirements for deployers

EU AI Act · Article 26
Human Oversight Obligation
If you deploy high-risk AI, you must ensure qualified humans can monitor and intervene in AI outputs. TBC-certified talent are trained specifically for this role.
GDPR · Articles 5 & 6
Lawful Data Processing
All TBC talent are GDPR-trained on lawful basis, data minimisation, and handling personal data. Every passport includes a GDPR badge.
GIPS Ethical Sourcing
Why Ethical Sourcing Protects You
Every TBC professional is sourced with transparent, auditable, ethical standards. If your company faces an EU AI Act audit, TBC provides full provenance documentation for every hire.
