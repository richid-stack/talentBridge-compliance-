Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 2026  ·  TalentBridge Compliance (TBC)

TalentBridge Compliance ("TBC", "we", "our", "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This policy explains what we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

1 Information We Collect

We may collect the following personal information when you use our platform:

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Phone number (WhatsApp)
  • Assessment and audit responses
  • Usage data — how you interact with the platform
  • Professional details such as your role, country, and company

2 How We Use Your Information

We use your data to:

  • Provide AI compliance audits and certification services
  • Issue and manage compliance certifications
  • Connect certified talent with global employers
  • Improve our platform, features, and services
  • Send important account and service notifications

3 Data Sharing

We do NOT sell your personal data. Your information will never be sold to third parties for marketing or advertising purposes.

We may share data only with:

  • Employers — only for certified talent visibility, with your consent
  • Trusted service providers — hosting, analytics, and operational tools under strict data processing agreements

4 Data Security

We implement reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your data from unauthorised access, loss, or misuse. Our platform uses Firebase authentication and encrypted data storage. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

5 Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

Request access to your data
Request deletion of your data
Request corrections or updates
Withdraw consent at any time
Request data portability
Object to certain processing

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the details in Section 8.

6 International Users

Our platform serves users globally, including professionals across Africa and deployers in Europe and beyond. By using TBC, you consent to your data being processed in accordance with this policy. We aim to comply with applicable data protection laws including the GDPR and relevant African data protection regulations.

7 Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes via email or a prominent notice on the platform. Continued use of TBC after updates constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
